# Connected V2X Roadside Network
- **ECU_V2V (V2X Module)**: Uses DSRC / C-V2X (802.11p) to broadcast BSMs (Basic Safety Messages) to surrounding vehicles and RSUs (Roadside Units).
- **Interface_RF (Antenna)**: The physical antenna array subject to jamming and spoofing.
- **Data_BSM (Basic Safety Message)**: Contains GPS coordinates, heading, and speed of the vehicle. Required to maintain traffic collision avoidance systems.
